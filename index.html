American Murder: Laci Peterson (2017)

In 2002, Laci Peterson, eight months pregnant, vanished from her home in California. The shocking case drew massive media attention and exposed flaws in public perception of guilt and innocence. The documentary examines the trial of Scott Peterson and the ongoing debate about what really happened.

Amy Bradley Is Missing (2025)

In 1998, 23-year-old Amy Lynn Bradley went missing during a family cruise in the Caribbean. Her sudden disappearance left behind only questions and turned into one of the most puzzling mysteries at sea. This documentary follows the search and the many unanswered theories surrounding her case.

American Murder: The Family Next Door (2020)

2018, Shanann Watts and her two daughters were reported missing in Colorado. As the search unfolded, it became clear that the family’s life was far from the perfect image they shared online. This Netflix documentary uses real footage and messages to show the hidden cracks behind closed doors.

Bad Surgeon: Love Under the Knife (2023)

Paolo Macchiarini was once a world-famous surgeon, admired for his bold ideas. He chose to perform experimental procedures that promised groundbreaking results — but soon, things did not go as planned. This Netflix series exposes how innovation and ambition turned into controversy.

Missing: The Lucie Blackman Case (2023)

In 2000, Lucie Blackman, a young British hostess, went missing in Tokyo, sparking an international investigation. The Netflix documentary revisits the case through family accounts and rare footage, exposing the dangers hidden in Japan’s nightlife.
